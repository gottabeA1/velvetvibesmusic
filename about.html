About Drake

Drake is a Canadian rapper, singer, and songwriter known for blending emotional R&B with modern hip-hop. He rose to fame with early mixtapes and became one of the most influential artists in the world.

Over his career, Drake has consistently evolved his sound, releasing chart-topping albums and shaping modern streaming culture. His music often explores themes of fame, relationships, ambition, and personal growth.
